Entropy:
 - Measure of unpredictability of state
 - In security, base two log of the number of guesses needed to find password with certainty
 - If password is KNOWN to come from list of 1024, then as 10 bits of entropy
 - Every bit of entropy doubles the cracking time
 - Per symbol:
   - Arabic numerals (0-9): 3.322 bits/symbol
   - (a-z)|(A-Z): 4.7 bits/symbol
   - Printable ASCII: 6.57 bits/symbol
   - Common word list (Diceware): 12.925 bits/symbol
